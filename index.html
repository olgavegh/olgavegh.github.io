My Story

Alright, buckle up and get ready for a ride! So, picture this: for a whopping 16 years, I was the mastermind behind the scenes as a library developer, shaping the Archicad program - the architect's ultimate design playground. Now, let me tell you, even though I was knee-deep in GDL language (which, by the way, is about as frontend as a pineapple on pizza), the hustle was real! I was juggling client demands, wrangling designs, planning projects, estimating like a pro, and making sure everything ran smoother than a baby's bottom.

But guess what? The frontend world was secretly calling my name all along! I just needed that 'aha' moment, and boy did I have it! All I was missing were the cool kids on the block - HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, you name it. Now, as a proud member of Generation Y, I might work like a seasoned Gen Xer, but my hunger for learning and riding the tech wave is straight out of the Gen Z playbook!

Sure, I could've stayed comfy in my old gig, but where's the fun in that? I decided to take a leap of faith, diving headfirst into the electrifying world of frontend development. And let me tell you, it's been one heck of a ride! Every day is a chance to soak up new knowledge, and I'm here for it, ready to tackle whatever tomorrow throws my way with a grin on my face!
